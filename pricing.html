Pricing FAQ

Question Answer
Can the current domain and email setup be kept? In most cases, yes. WayLight normally works with your existing domain and mail provider where practical.
Do you add analytics and cookies by default? No. Analytics and cookie tooling are added only where needed for your goals and compliance context.
How quickly will a reply be received? Initial reply is typically within 1 business day.
What if the right package is not clear? Request a short call and WayLight will recommend the simplest workable option.

Getting started: To begin a review of your requirements, WayLight needs your services in plain language, any existing branding, contact details and preferred call-to-action, and any required compliance text.

Response expectation: Initial reply within 1 business day. The priority is always simple, maintainable systems rather than fragile setups.
