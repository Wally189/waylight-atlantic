How pricing works

Clear structure before detail

Groups Services

Website work is listed separately from digital organisation, administration, and governance support. This keeps pricing simple and easier to scan although Waylight can offer these as a package.

Fixed packages or quoted work

Some services have clear fixed packages, while broader work is quoted based on scope. This keeps pricing fair and avoids unnecessary costs. Waylight is here to support small and independent business keep costs down.

Support options available

Ongoing support packages are available for organisations that want help keeping their website or digital systems updated over time. Alternative we have packages where we provide the website and you manage the content.
