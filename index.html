What “good” looks like

A website that loads quickly, reads clearly, answers the obvious questions, and nudges people to act. Behind the scenes: files and routines that don’t collapse when someone goes on annual leave.

  • Simple navigation, proper page hierarchy
  • Clear pricing signals (no awkward mystery)
  • Contact paths that actually get replies
  • Basic compliance hygiene (privacy, cookies as needed)
  • Handovers that prevent “knowledge hoarding”
